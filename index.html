The Process Flow

The most Convenient Liquor Delivery System In The Vicinity

Create your account

Create an account on our app by using your phone number and filling your basic details. After filling your personal details you have to show a valid age verification document. Enter your address properly to get a hassle-free delivery. If you already have an account, enter your registered phone number and log into your account.

Browse Premium Liquors

Browse our store's extensive selection of alcoholic beverages. From whiskey to wine and beer to absinthe, everything is accessible with a single click. You can purchase every type of alcohol in various quantities for a fair price. All you need to do is scroll through those choices and place your favorites in the shopping cart.

Review Your Cart

Now that you have handpicked your booze and some finger-licking add-ons from our vast array of collections, it's now time to review all the items that you have added to your cart. This is when you should take your time gazing at the added items in your cart. Once you are done and fully satisfied with your selections of the day, it's time for the next step!

Select Add-ons Available at Your Nearby Stores

Did you really think we would be showing up just to hand over your choice of alcohol? Well, here's a fun fact! Along with your booze, you can order some delicious snacks and other add-ons from nearby shops. This is a one-stop destination to make your weekends, parties, and gatherings extra happening.

Place Your Order

Select all the products you want and add them to your cart. Fill up the address form with your house number, street name, and other details so that we can deliver your order right at your doorstep. After filling in the address details, proceed to checkout. Check the prices and then pay by cash on delivery. You will get a confirmation message after placing your order.

You Order, We Deliver

Once you have surpassed all the steps for creating an account, selecting your booze, and some finger-licking add-ons, it's now time to place your order and simply relax as your work is done and now it's our turn. You can track your order and it will be delivered to your doorstep as fast as possible.
